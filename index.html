About me

Welcome to my personal website. I am Amal Vincent, an interdisciplinary researcher in the field of Human Computer Interaction, residing in Vancouver, Canada.

I am currently a graduate student at School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University, and work in the BioViz lab with Dr. Chris Shaw. I have strong ties with electical engineering and computer science. My current research is in the domain of Health Informatics with an emphasis on Artificial Intelligence, Visual Analytics and, sensory technology.
