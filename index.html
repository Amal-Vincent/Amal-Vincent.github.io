About me

Welcome to my personal website. I am Amal Vincent, an interdisciplinary researcher in the field of Human Computer Interaction, currently residing in Vancouver, Canada.

I am presently a graduate student at School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University, and work in the BioViz lab with Dr. Chris Shaw. I have strong ties with electical engineering and computer science, my research has always dealt with the same. My current research is in the domain of Health Informatics with an emphasis on Artificial Intelligence, Visual Analytics and, sensory technology.
