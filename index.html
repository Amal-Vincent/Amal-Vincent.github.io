Amal Vincent

Computer Scientist

Welcome to my website. I am Amal Vincent, an interdisciplinary researcher in the domain of Health Informatics, residing in Vancouver, Canada. I grew up on the southwestern coast of India, in Kochi — a fun place to visit if you love to travel.

I am currently a graduate student at the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University and work in the BioViz lab with Dr. Chris Shaw. I am a computer scientist, an engineer, a designer and a social activist.

Research

My current work is in the domain of health informatics with significant contributions in the field of Data Analytics, Visual Analytics and Artificial Intelligence. Our lab- the BioViz lab headed by Dr. Chris Shaw, currently focuses on studying behavioural changes induced by medical technology (especially with regards to patient self-monitoring technologies).
